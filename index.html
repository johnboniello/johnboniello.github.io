Extended Time Calculator

A tool for special education teachers.

The extended time calculator is a tool to help special education teachers calculate the time a student with a disability actually needs when taking a test.   Far too often teachers and others simply guess at the amount of time a student might need for testing accommodations.   This guessing is not accurate and we are not providing students with the actual time that they need on tests.   With this tool teachers can keep track of the time a student takes on a test vs the time that was expeted of all students and calculate the accommodation.   This tool must be used in conjunction with other data and information to make this decision.
